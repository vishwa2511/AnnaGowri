Hospital

OPD and IPD Departments and Facilities

OPD’s of all clinical departments exist in the hospital in the well equipped 100 bedded Hospital where patients of various disorders are managed with ayurvedic therapy by specialists of respective fields. A well equipped operation theatre, labour room, pathological lab, X Ray, E.E.G, ECG, kshar sutra clinic and pancha–karma clinic render services and facilitate to the needs of patients.

Patients of various chronic ailments like Osteo-Arthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Gouty Arthriti, Asthma and bronchial Allergy, Ascites, Diabetes, Intestinal Disorder, Liver Diseases, Heart Diseases, Epilepsy Mal-Nutrition related diseases of the children, Infertlity, Skin Disorders, Leucorrhoea and Order Gynaecological Diseases, Fracture and Dislocation, Fistula in Ano, Fissure in Ano, Piles, Anal Prolapse, Chronic Ulcers, Urinary Disorders, Musculo Skeletall Disorders and Autoimmune Diseases etc are managed effectively with Ayurveda.

Hospital Infrastructure

  • OPD outdoor patient department
  • IPD - indoor patient department-115 beds General and Private wards for male, female & children
  • OT - 1 Major OT: 2 separate Minor OTs

Investigation Unit

  • Radiology : Machines for X-Ray and USG 2. Pathology Laboratory
  • Monitoring system- N.I.B.P, Multi-paraoxymeter, Finger-Tip Pulse oxymeter, Boyle apparatus etc.

Shalya OPD/IPD

  • It consist of 3 major facilities:
  • OPD ,IPD, Operation theaters.
  • Several surgical procedures like Hernia, Hydrocoele, Cholecystectomy, Appendicectomy, Perforation, Prostatectomy, Anorectal surgeries etc. are performed routinely by skilled surgeons.
  • Our Shalyatantra OPD/IPD has well equipped and maintained operation theatres for major and minor operative procedures.

Panchkarma OPD/IPD

  • Vamana Karma (Therapeutic Emesis)
  • Virechana Karma (Therapeutic Purgation)
  • Anuvasana Vasti (Therapeutic Oil Enema)
  • Nasya Karma (Nasal Medication)
  • Nasya Karma (Nasal Medication)

Kayachikitsa OPD/IPD

Here is conduting various activities in the evaluation of efficacy of ayurvedic drugs in udar roga (gastrointestinal disorders), yakrit roga (hepatology), hrid roga (cardio-respiratory disorders), twak roga (dermatology & VD), manas roga (psychiatry), rasayana and vajikarana (geriatrics and aphrodisiacs) and vata vyadhi (rheumatology and neurology).

Shalakya OPD/IPD

Diseases related to parts lying above clavicles i.e. ears, eyes, mouth, nose etc. are dealt with & treated,and method of using Shlaka (probe) is mentioned is called as Shalakya Tantra

Clinical Facilities

  • Diagnosis and treatment of disorders related with Shalakya
  • Refraction
  • Ocular Physiotherapy specially Yoga & eye sight
  • Kriya Kalpa (Ayurvedic topical administration of drugs)
  • Magnetotherapy, Acupressure and Acupuncture.

Stri prasuti OPD/IPD

  • Rotatory OPD duties from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. Three days in the week for screening & vaccination of ANCs
  • Admissions are open in routine O.P.D. & emergency also as per need for condition of patient
  • Counseling for diet regimen especially for ANCs and also for case of infertility and contraception is done
  • All kind of therapies e.g. Uttarbasti, Pichu, Dhawan, Dahan are provided
  • ANCs are registered under Jannani Suraksha Yojana
  • IUCD (Cu T) is applied as per need.

BAL ROGA OPD/IPD

Due to parallel support of our Bal Roga Department, Neonatal Care and all vaccination are also provided.

Swastham OPD:

Yoga classes are conducted in our campus for better health. This opd includes nutrition and dietetics.daily diet and regimens to patients is planned.awareness camps, health statistics of patients in nearby villages are taken care by this OPD.

Atyayika OPD:

There is a fully functional 24 hours (Round the clock) emergency service having all basic required equipments, Instruments, Medicines with proper management to the patient during the time of need.
