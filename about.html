Why School of Anna Gowri Ayurveda?

Anna Gowri Ayurveda College Hospitals & Research Centre

The ANNA GOWRI AYURVEDA COLLEGE HOSPITALS & RESEARCH CENTRE is situated in the TIRUTTANI-TIRUPATI Highway in the divine land of ANDHRA PRADESH. The campus is easily approachable by road and Indian railway network. The College is functioning under the aegis of ANNA GOWRI EDUCATIONAL TRUST with the objective to provide patient care education, research and training to aspiring youth and providing quality higher education.

Vision

To be a premier stake holder focusing on Quality Healthcare, professional education, research and patient care.

Mission

To build an institution of global standard and repute for imparting quality Ayurveda education by excellent teaching and training and positively sustainable health care as well as undertake research activities to reach newer horizons.
