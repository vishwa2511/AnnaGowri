Why School of Anna Gowri Ayurveda?

Anna Gowri Ayurveda College Hospitals & Research Centre

The ANNA GOWRI AYURVEDA COLLEGE HOSPITALS & RESEARCH CENTRE is situated in the TIRUTTANI-TIRUPATI Highway in the divine land of ANDHRA PRADESH. The campus is easily approachable by road and Indian railway network. The College is functioning under the aegis of ANNA GOWRI EDUCATIONAL TRUST with the objective to provide patient care education, research and training to aspiring youth and providing quality higher education.

Vision

  • Several surgical procedures like Hernia, Hydrocoele, Cholecystectomy, Appendicectomy, Perforation, Prostatectomy, Anorectal surgeries etc. are performed routinely by skilled surgeons.
  • Our Shalyatantra OPD/IPD has well equipped and maintained operation theatres for major and minor operative procedures.

Words from CHAIRMAN

Anna Gowri Hospitals started functioning as a hospital primarily providing health care to the needy. The doctors, nurses, and other health care practitioners, who in their own way, and at their own time, continue to further the mission as a service to the humanity.

Anna Gowri Hospitals engages the community by performing rural health camps continuously. We have active Departments that works in the community for preventive and social medicine.

We have come a long way, but have to travel far. Teaching hospitals world over are always at the cutting edge of medical care and research. They are considered the best in medical care and practice. We must be no different. We have to be considered the best in medical care, while at the same time, staying true to our mission, of providing health care to those who need it most, and also to those who can least afford it.

Our research should be such that strives to find the cures of tomorrow, and the care should be such that provides the best comfort of today. Our faculties must be inspired to follow in the dreams of our founder. This is our aim, and these are now our dreams.

Words from CEO (Ayurveda)

We have started this Hospital with a clear vision in mind to impart world class medical treatment for our patients. We understand the importance of a holistic approach to health, which is why we also offer a range of wellness programs and activities aimed at promoting physical and emotional well-being.

Whether you are seeking relief from a specific health condition or simply looking to improve your overall health and well-being, Ayurveda is based on the principle of balancing the three doshas - Vata, Pitta, and Kapha - which are the three fundamental energies that govern our physical, mental, and emotional well-being. With the high stressful work environment and unhealthy eating habits, Ayurveda is more needed to the mankind than any other time since its origin.

We build a strong platform to make this science beneficial to the needy world with authentic and quality.

CEO (Ayurveda)
